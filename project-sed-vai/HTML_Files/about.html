Purpose

We aim to provide the most comprehensive database of music information on the internet, including information on artists, albums, songs, and more. Our goal is to help music lovers discover new artists and explore new genres.

We are grateful to Prof. Anoop Namboodiri and the TAs of the Introduction to Software Systems course for providing us with the resources and oppurtunity to work on this project.

What it contains

As mentioned above, the site is an interactive(To be implemented) database of Songs where each user can rate Songs while also receiving suggestions based on ratings provided by other users

What it can be Used For

We hope that this tool will help people find new music rated by others with similar tastes so that they can explore what they like in a more refined manner.
